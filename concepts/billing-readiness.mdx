Billing readiness is Affretio's completeness check for a transport dossier. A
record can advance in pre-invoicing only when every required operational and
commercial condition is satisfied.

## Readiness conditions

```text
Delivered transport
       +
Effective price
       +
Required delivery proof
       +
No unresolved blocking incident
       =
Ready for pre-invoicing
```

## Effective price

Affretio can use the applicable effective price for the transport. Depending on
the record, this may come from an explicit manual price or configured valuation.
Distance and tariff-grid scope must be complete when automatic valuation is expected.

## Delivery proof

When proof is required, the dossier stays incomplete until the receipt exists.
The receipt may contain the recipient's identity, capacity, signature outcome,
time, and reservations.

## Blocking incidents

Acknowledging an alert does not automatically resolve the operational issue behind
it. Update the real transport or incident state before expecting readiness to change.

## Readiness is not invoice status

Readiness answers whether the dossier is complete. Invoice status answers where it
is in the financial workflow: **À préparer**, **Approuvé**, **Exporté**, or **Facturé**.
A ready dossier may still be Pending until an administrator reviews it.

<Warning>
  Never change operational truth only to obtain a ready badge. Correct the missing
  source information or resolve the real issue.
</Warning>
