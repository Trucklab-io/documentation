Trucklab permissions follow the least-access principle. Give each person the role
needed for their work and no more.

## Role summary

| Capability | Owner | Admin | Regular |
| --- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| View operational workspace data | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Create and edit operational resources | Yes | Yes | When permitted by the workflow |
| Work with planning drafts | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Submit planning for review | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Approve or reject planning | Yes | Yes | No |
| Manage workspace settings | Yes | Yes | No |
| Create or remove workspace users | Yes | No | No |
| Control workspace ownership | Yes | No | No |

<Note>
  The interface and backend enforce permissions. A hidden or disabled action usually
  means your current role cannot perform it in the selected workspace.
</Note>

## Owner

Owners have full access. Use this role for the person responsible for the Trucklab
workspace and its membership.

Owners can:

- maintain the company profile and workspace settings;
- create and remove user access;
- manage operational data;
- review, approve, reject, and restore planning versions.

Keep at least one active Owner. Limit the role to people who need membership and
workspace-control permissions.

## Admin

Admins manage operational settings and can review planning. Use this role for a
transport manager, senior dispatcher, or another person responsible for published schedules.

Admins can approve or reject planning, but user membership remains an Owner responsibility.

## Regular

Regular users work with operational information and draft planning. Use this role
for dispatchers and other team members who prepare work for review.

Regular users can submit a draft but cannot approve their own or another user's
planning version.

## Change access safely

Before changing a user's role:

1. Confirm the selected workspace.
2. Confirm the user's email address.
3. Check whether they have planning waiting for review.
4. Apply the least privileged role that supports their responsibilities.
5. Ask them to sign in again if the updated permissions do not appear immediately.

<Warning>
  Removing the last active Owner can leave the workspace without a user who can
  manage membership. Confirm another Owner exists before changing Owner access.
</Warning>
