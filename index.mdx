Affretio is Trucklab's dispatch workspace for transport teams. Use it to keep
operational resources together, build daily planning, and publish reviewed schedules.

<Note>
  These guides cover the customer-facing product. The Trucklab API is private and
  is not included in this documentation.
</Note>

## How the workspace fits together

Your company profile owns the drivers and trucks used in operations. Clients are
the organizations you plan trips for. Each planning is scoped to one client and
one period, with version history protecting published work.
