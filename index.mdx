Affretio is Trucklab's dispatch workspace for transport teams. Use it to maintain
clients and fleet resources, build reviewed planning, follow transport execution,
share progress, and prepare completed work for invoicing.

<Note>
  These guides cover the customer-facing product. The Trucklab API is private and
  is not included in this documentation.
</Note>

## How the workspace fits together

Your company profile owns the drivers and trucks used in operations. Clients are
the organizations you plan trips for. Planning versions protect published work.
Operations then turns delivered trips into trackable, reviewable, and billable work.
