Complete this checklist to make the workspace ready for daily planning.

## Before you begin

- You need an active Trucklab user.
- An **Owner** should complete company and access settings.
- Keep your company address and the first vehicle registration available.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in and confirm the workspace">
    Sign in to Affretio. If a workspace selector appears, choose the workspace you
    intend to configure.

    Check the workspace name on **Home** before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the company profile">
    Open **Settings**, select **Company**, then find **Own company profile**.

    Enter the company name and, when available, the street address, city, postal
    code, and country. Save the profile.

    <Note>
      A company profile is required before you can add trucks.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the first client">
    Open **Home**, select **Open details** for Clients, and select **New client**.

    Complete **Basics**, **Location**, and **Review**, then select **Create client**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the first driver">
    Return to **Home**, open Drivers, and select **New driver**.

    Enter the driver's identity, contact details, and preferred truck type. Review
    the information and create the driver.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the first truck">
    Return to **Home**, open Trucks, and select **New truck**.

    Enter the registration and truck type. You can assign the driver now or leave
    the truck unassigned. Add lifecycle information when available, then create the truck.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the Home summary">
    Open **Home** and select **Refresh**. Confirm that the client, driver, and truck
    counts match the records you created.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review settings and operations access">
    If you are the Owner, open **Settings → Users** and confirm the membership list.
    Owners and Admins should also confirm that **Operations** appears in the sidebar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## You are ready when

- Your company name appears under **Settings → Company**.
- At least one client appears in Clients.
- At least one driver appears in Drivers.
- At least one truck appears in Trucks.
- The correct users and roles appear under **Settings → Users**.

<Tip>
  You can create a planning with unassigned trips, but adding drivers and trucks
  first makes scheduling faster.
</Tip>
