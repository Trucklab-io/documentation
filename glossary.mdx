## A

**Account**  
A backend and occasional UI term for the active customer scope. In these guides,
we usually call it a workspace.

**Active planning**  
The published planning currently accepted for operations.

**Affrètement**
A subcontracting record that offers one approved transport to a carrier partner.

**Alert**
An Operations record for a late, blocked, unacknowledged, or incomplete transport.

**Admin**  
A workspace role that manages operational data and can review planning versions.

**Affretio**  
Trucklab's dispatch workspace for clients, drivers, trucks, and planning.

**Approved**  
A planning-version status showing that an Owner or Admin accepted and published the version.

## C

**Carrier**
A partner transport company that can receive an affrètement offer.

**Client**  
An organization that the transport company plans trips for.

## D

**Draft**  
An editable planning version that has not been published.

**Driver**  
A person available for operational assignment to trips and trucks.

**Driver absence**  
A date range during which a driver is unavailable for assignment.

**Delivery receipt**
Operational proof containing the delivery outcome and, when required, recipient details.

**Effective price**
The price used by Affretio for billing readiness, based on the transport's available valuation data.

**ETA**
Estimated time of arrival calculated from planning time, duration, and unresolved delay. It is not GPS tracking.

## O

**Operations**
The Owner- and Admin-only area for pre-invoicing, alerts, carriers, charters, tracking, and ETA review.

**Owner**  
The highest workspace role. Owners manage users, settings, operational data, and planning review.

## P

**Pre-invoice**
A transport billing dossier that moves through Pending, Approved, Exported, and Invoiced states.

**Planning**  
The collection of trips scheduled for a client and period.

**Planning history**  
The searchable record of Draft, Submitted, Approved, and Rejected planning versions.

**Planning version**  
A numbered snapshot of planning entries used for editing, review, publication, and restore.

## R

**Recurring trip**  
A daily, weekly, or monthly template that generates trips when a matching planning is opened.

**Regular user**  
An operational role that works with assigned data and draft planning but does not approve planning.

**Rejected**  
A submitted planning version that a reviewer did not accept.

**Restore**  
The action that creates a new version based on an earlier approved snapshot without rewriting history.

## S

**Submitted**  
A planning-version status indicating the draft is waiting for Owner or Admin review.

## T

**Tariff grid**
A set of client-, truck-, and distance-scoped pricing bands used for trip valuation.

**Tracking share**
A time-limited, revocable secret link that exposes selected transport progress to a client.

**Trip**  
A planned movement between departure and arrival addresses, optionally assigned to a driver and truck.

**Truck**  
A fleet vehicle identified by registration and truck type, with an operational status and optional driver.

**Trucklab**  
The company and documentation brand for the Affretio product.

## W

**Workspace**  
The customer environment containing company settings, users, operational resources, and planning.
