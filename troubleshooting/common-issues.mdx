title: "Common issues"
description: "Resolve frequent access, workspace, resource, and planning problems."

---

Start with the checks below before changing or recreating operational data.

## I cannot sign in

1. Confirm the email address used for Trucklab access.
2. Check Caps Lock and try the password again.
3. Use the password-reset flow.
4. Ask an Owner to confirm that your user still appears in **Access list**.
5. If you have access to multiple workspaces, confirm you are opening the expected one.

Do not create a duplicate user as the first recovery step.

## An action is hidden or disabled

The most common causes are:

- your role cannot perform the action;
- no workspace is selected;
- required setup data is missing;
- another operation is still loading;
- the selected planning version has the wrong status for the action.

Check [Roles and permissions](/concepts/roles-and-permissions), then refresh the workspace.

## I cannot create a truck

Open **Dashboard** and complete **Own company profile**. Trucks must be associated
with the operating company, so **New truck** remains disabled until that profile exists.

## I cannot create or publish planning

Confirm that:

- at least one client exists;
- a client is selected in Planning;
- a planning day is selected;
- you are viewing a Draft before selecting **Soumettre**;
- you are an Owner or Admin before selecting **Approuver** or **Rejeter**.

## A driver cannot be assigned

Open **Drivers** and review the driver's availability dates. Planning blocks an
assignment when the trip date overlaps a recorded absence.

Correct an incorrect absence, choose another driver, or create the trip with
**Assigner plus tard**.

## A trip form will not save

Check the required fields:

- trip day;
- complete departure address;
- complete arrival address.

If an arrival time is present, enter a departure time. Departure and arrival time
cannot be identical for the same schedule.

## My changes are not active

Open **Actions** and check the selected planning version. Draft and Submitted versions
are not published. An Owner or Admin must approve the version before it becomes active.

Use **Planning history** to confirm the latest status.

## A recurring trip did not appear

1. Confirm the template is **Active**.
2. Confirm the selected client matches the template.
3. Check the recurrence days and start or end dates.
4. Open Planning for a matching date; occurrences are created when the planning is loaded.
5. Search the planning before creating another template or trip.

## Counts or lists look outdated

Return to **Home** and select **Refresh**. If the issue remains, reload the browser
page and verify the selected workspace.

## A client, driver, or truck cannot be deleted

The record may still be referenced by planning, recurring trips, assignments, or
history. Prefer editing or deactivating the operational record when possible.

<Warning>
  Do not delete related planning just to remove a referenced resource. Preserve
  operational history and ask a workspace Owner to review the relationship.
</Warning>

## Report a documentation problem

If the product behavior differs from these guides, open a
[documentation issue](https://github.com/Trucklab-io/documentation/issues/new) with:

- the page URL;
- the step that no longer matches;
- the visible UI label or error;
- a screenshot with customer and personal data removed.
