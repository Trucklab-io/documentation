Start with the smallest check that can explain the problem. Avoid deleting or
recreating operational data until you understand its references.

## I cannot sign in

1. Confirm the exact email address used for workspace access.
2. Check Caps Lock and enter the password again.
3. Select **Forgot password?** and complete the reset flow.
4. Ask an Owner to confirm your record under **Settings → Users → Access list**.
5. Ask the Owner to confirm that your access is **Active**.

Do not select **Create workspace** as a sign-in recovery step.

## An action or page is hidden

Check your role first. Common examples:

- only an Owner can edit the company profile or manage workspace users;
- only Owners and Admins can approve or reject planning;
- **Operations** is available only to Owners and Admins;
- an action can be unavailable for the current version or invoice status.

Sign out and back in after a confirmed role change.

## Counts or lists look outdated

- Select **Refresh** on Home, Dashboard, History, or Operations when available.
- Clear search and filters.
- Check the selected page and pagination size.
- Confirm that you are in the intended workspace.
- Reload the browser page if a live connection did not recover.

## I cannot create a truck

An Owner must complete **Settings → Company → Own company profile**. Confirm the
operating company, registration, and truck type in **Basics**.

## A driver cannot be assigned

Open Drivers and review current and future absences. Also confirm the driver belongs
to the operating organization and the associated truck is not in Maintenance.
Choose another driver or use **Assigner plus tard** when the absence is correct.

## A trip form will not save

Confirm:

- trip day;
- complete departure and arrival addresses;
- departure time when an arrival time is present;
- different departure and arrival times for the same schedule;
- valid numeric load, distance, duration, or price values;
- a compatible driver and truck when assignment is required.

## I cannot submit, approve, reject, or restore

| Action | Required state | Required role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Soumettre** | Draft | User who can work with planning |
| **Approuver** | Submitted | Owner or Admin |
| **Rejeter** | Submitted | Owner or Admin |
| **Restaurer** | Appropriate approved snapshot | Owner or Admin |

Reconfirm the client, date, and version badge. Use Planning history to open the exact snapshot.

## My planning changes are not active

Draft and Submitted versions are not published. An Owner or Admin must approve the
version. Use Planning history to confirm the newest status and version number.

## A recurring trip did not appear

1. Confirm the template is **Active**.
2. Confirm the Planning client matches the template.
3. Check frequency, allowed weekdays or monthly day, and date boundaries.
4. Open Planning for a matching date to trigger generation.
5. Clear filters and search before creating a duplicate.

## A client, driver, truck, or carrier cannot be deleted

The record may be referenced by planning, history, recurring trips, tariff grids,
assignments, charters, tracking shares, or billing work. Prefer editing, pausing,
deactivating, or marking unavailable when the record must remain for history.

## Operations is unavailable

Confirm you are an Owner or Admin. If the page is visible but data does not update,
check the live indicator and use refresh. Approved planning must exist before trips
can appear in post-planning operations.

## A pre-invoice is blocked

Open the dossier and read the blocker. Confirm the transport is delivered, a price
exists, required proof is signed, and blocking incidents are resolved. See
[Billing readiness](/concepts/billing-readiness).

## A tracking link does not work

- Copy the complete newest URL.
- Check whether its state is Expired or Revoked.
- Confirm the intended transport and client.
- Create a replacement only when access is still appropriate.

Never paste the secret URL into a public issue.

## A route has no ETA

The planned trip needs a date, departure time, and duration. Correct the Planning
record and confirm any latest delay. Routes & ETA is planning-based, not GPS-based.

## Report a documentation problem

Open a [documentation issue](https://github.com/Trucklab-io/documentation/issues/new) with:

- the documentation page URL;
- the exact step that differs;
- the visible UI label or safe error text;
- your role and relevant version status;
- a screenshot with customer, personal, financial, vehicle, and secret-link data removed.
