Clients are the organizations your transport company plans trips for. You need at
least one client before you can create or publish a planning.

## Create a client

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Clients">
    Open **Home**, find Clients, and select **Open details**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a new client">
    Select **New client**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the basics">
    In **Basics**, enter the client name. This name appears in planning selectors,
    recurring trips, history, and exports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the location">
    In **Location**, enter the street address, country, city, and postal code when available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and create">
    Check the summary in **Review**, confirm the information is correct, and select
    **Create client**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use the client's operational name—the name dispatchers expect to see while planning.
</Tip>

## Edit a client

1. Open **Clients**.
2. Find the client using the search field.
3. Open the row actions and choose edit.
4. Update the relevant fields.
5. Review the change and select **Save client**.

Changes to the client name appear anywhere that client is referenced. Existing
planning history remains associated with the same client record.

## Find and review clients

Use **Search clients** to filter by client information. You can show 15, 25, or 50
clients per page when reviewing a larger directory.

## Before deleting a client

Check for associated planning, recurring trips, and tariff grids. Trucklab may
reject deletion when the client is still referenced by operational data.

<Warning>
  Do not delete and recreate a client just to correct its name or address. Edit the
  existing client so its planning history stays connected.
</Warning>
