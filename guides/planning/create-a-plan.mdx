title: "Create a planning"
description: "Create client trips, assign drivers and trucks, and prepare a draft schedule."

Use Planning to build the operational schedule for one client and day. Your edits
are stored in a planning version so the team can review them before publication.

## Before you begin

- Create the client you are planning for.
- Add the drivers and trucks you expect to assign.
- Record known driver absences.
- Confirm the active workspace.

## Open the planning

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Planning">
    Select **Planning** in the main navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a client">
    Select a client organization. If there are no clients, use **Ajouter un client**
    and complete the client setup first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the day">
    Use **Jour du planning** or the previous and next day buttons to open the date
    you want to schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create a trip

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the trip">
    Open **Trajets** and select **Créer un trajet**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the day">
    Enter the trip day. This field is required and cannot be changed after the trip is created.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign resources or plan later">
    Choose a driver and truck, or use **Assigner plus tard** to create an unassigned trip.

    An unavailable driver cannot be assigned for a date covered by an absence.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the schedule">
    Add departure and arrival times when known. If you enter an arrival time, you
    must also enter a departure time. Overnight arrivals are displayed with a day offset.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the route">
    Provide complete departure and arrival addresses. Both addresses are required.
    Choose an address suggestion when one matches the location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add operational details">
    Add load, distance, manual price, and status when available. New trips start as **Planifié**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the trip">
    Save the form. The trip appears in the draft planning version.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Assign trips on the board

You can assign or move trips from the planning board after creation.

- Use **Mes camions disponibles** to find an appropriate truck.
- Search by driver, city, registration, or route.
- Use the insert or move controls to place a trip in a driver's schedule.
- Resolve any driver availability or timing warning before continuing.

## Use planning statuses

| Status | Use it when |
| --- | --- |
| **Planifié** | The trip is scheduled but not yet confirmed. |
| **Confirmé** | The assignment and route are confirmed. |
| **Livré** | The trip has been completed. |
| **Annulé** | The trip will not be operated. |

## Check the draft

Before submission:

- Search for unassigned trips.
- Confirm each driver is available.
- Check truck status and overlapping times.
- Verify complete departure and arrival addresses.
- Confirm the client and planning day.

<Tip>
  Use **Imprimer** or **PDF** in **Actions** to review the visible planning outside
  the board. Exporting does not publish the planning.
</Tip>
