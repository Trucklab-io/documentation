After creating trips, use the Planning board to resolve assignments, timing, and
operational status before review.

## Assign a trip

- Open an unassigned trip from **Trajets**.
- Choose an available driver and truck.
- Use the schedule controls to insert or move work into the correct driver timeline.
- Recheck the route and timing after moving a trip.

Affretio validates driver availability and linked resources. A truck in Maintenance
or a driver covered by an absence should not be treated as available.

## Search and filter

Use **Rechercher un chauffeur ou trajet...** to match driver or trip information.
The status filter includes:

| UI status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Planifié** | Scheduled but not yet confirmed. |
| **Confirmé** | Assignment and route are confirmed. |
| **Livré** | Completed transport. |
| **Annulé** | Transport will not operate. |

Use **Tous les statuts** to return to the complete planning.

## Adjust the board view

- Toggle **Trajets** to show or hide the trip panel.
- Use **Dézoomer** and **Zoomer** for the timeline scale.
- Choose a density preset when you need a faster overview or more timing detail.
- Use the previous and next day arrows to move the planning date.

## Edit one trip

Open the trip detail panel and select edit. Recheck all fields after changing a
driver, truck, time, route, distance, price, or status. Preserve the trip record
instead of deleting and recreating it for a simple correction.

## Update several trips

Select visible trips to open the bulk toolbar. You can choose a status and select
**Mettre à jour**, select all visible rows, or delete the selection when your role permits.

<Warning>
  Bulk changes apply to the selected trips, including selections that may be outside
  your immediate visual focus. Confirm the selection count, client, day, and version first.
</Warning>

## Review real-time execution

Approved planning can display a live connection state such as **Temps réel connecté**,
**Reconnexion temps réel…**, or **Synchronisation indisponible**. This connection
updates execution information; it does not replace validation of assignments or route data.

## Print or export

Open **Actions** and choose **Imprimer** or **PDF**. The report contains the selected
client, version context, driver, truck, schedule, route, load, and status for visible work.

Exporting does not approve or publish a planning version. Apply the required filters
before export and review the generated file before sharing it.

## Pre-submission checklist

- No unexpected unassigned trips remain.
- Drivers are available and timing does not overlap.
- Trucks have a usable status and the intended type.
- Departure and arrival addresses are complete.
- Times and overnight offsets are correct.
- Distance, load, manual price, and status are accurate when known.
- The client, planning day, and version are correct.
