Planning versions protect the active schedule from unreviewed edits. Regular users
can prepare and submit drafts. Owners and Admins can review and publish them.

## Submit a draft

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the correct planning">
    In **Planning**, confirm the client and planning day.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the draft version">
    Open **Actions** and use the version selector. A draft is shown with its version
    number and `draft` status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the operational review">
    Check assignments, driver availability, truck status, route addresses, times,
    and trip statuses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the version">
    Select **Soumettre**. The version moves from Draft to Submitted and appears in
    the review queue for an Owner or Admin.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  A submitted version is not yet the active published planning.
</Warning>

## Approve a version

You need the **Owner** or **Admin** role.

1. Open the submitted planning from the review queue or **Planning history**.
2. Confirm the client, period, and version number.
3. Review the complete trip list.
4. Open **Actions** and select **Approuver**.
5. Confirm that the approved version is now the active planning.

Approval publishes the version's entries into the active operational planning.

## Reject a version

If the planning needs correction, open **Actions** and select **Rejeter**. The
rejected version stays in history. Create or continue with a new draft to make the
required corrections, then submit it for another review.

## Restore an earlier version

Use restore when a previous approved snapshot should become the basis for new work.

1. Open **Planning history** and find the approved version.
2. Open the version in Planning.
3. Open **Actions** and select **Restaurer**.
4. Review the new version created from that snapshot.
5. Submit and approve it through the normal workflow.

<Note>
  Restore creates another version. It does not rewrite or erase planning history.
</Note>

## Review statuses

| Status | Meaning | Next action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Draft** | Editable work that is not published. | Finish checks and submit. |
| **Submitted** | Waiting for Owner or Admin review. | Approve or reject. |
| **Approved** | Published as an accepted planning version. | Operate it or restore it later. |
| **Rejected** | Reviewed but not accepted. | Correct the planning in a new draft. |

## Use Planning history

Open **Planning history** to search by date, route, or client. Filter by client and
status to audit the latest planning versions. The history view is the safest place
to confirm whether work is still a draft or has been approved.
