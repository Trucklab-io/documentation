Planning versions separate editing from the schedule accepted for operations.
Regular users prepare and submit drafts. Owners and Admins review and publish them.

## Submit a draft

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the exact planning">
    Confirm the client, day, and version badge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the draft">
    Check every assignment, absence, truck status, time, address, load, distance,
    price, trip status, and unassigned item.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Open **Actions** and select **Soumettre**. The status changes from Draft to Submitted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Submitted means ready for review. It does not mean published or active.
</Warning>

## Approve a version

You need Owner or Admin access.

1. Open the submitted version from Planning or **Planning history**.
2. Confirm the client, period, version number, submitter, and change summary.
3. Review the complete trip snapshot.
4. Open **Actions** and select **Approuver**.
5. Confirm that the Approved version is now the active planning.

Approval publishes the version entries into the active operational planning.

## Reject a version

Select **Rejeter** when the submitted snapshot needs correction. The Rejected
version stays in history. Correct the work in a new or continued Draft, then submit
that version for another review.

## Review status matrix

| Status | Editable | Published | Who acts next |
| --- | :---: | :---: | --- |
| **Draft** | Yes | No | Author finishes and submits. |
| **Submitted** | No normal editing | No | Owner or Admin approves or rejects. |
| **Approved** | Snapshot retained | Yes | Operations uses it; reviewer may restore later. |
| **Rejected** | Snapshot retained | No | Author prepares corrected work. |

## Reviewer checklist

- Correct client, date, and version.
- No accidental duplicate or missing trip.
- Every assignment is feasible.
- Driver absence and truck status are respected.
- Times, overnight arrivals, and routes are credible.
- Manual prices and unusual statuses are intentional.
- Exported view matches the expected operating schedule.

## Direct administrative changes

Owners and Admins may have actions that update planning directly. Affretio keeps
version history aligned, but you should still verify the active version after any
administrative change.
