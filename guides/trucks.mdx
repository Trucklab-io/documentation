Truck records represent the vehicles dispatchers assign to trips. Each record has
a registration, type, optional driver, operational status, and lifecycle details.

## Before you begin

Complete **Own company profile** on the Dashboard. Affretio disables **New truck**
until your operating company exists.

## Create a truck

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Trucks">
    Open **Home**, find Trucks, and select **Open details**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a truck record">
    Select **New truck**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the basics">
    In **Basics**, enter the registration and choose the truck type.

    Use a consistent registration format so dispatchers can find the vehicle quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the assignment and status">
    In **Assignment**, choose a driver or leave the truck **Unassigned**. Set its
    current status to **Available**, **On route**, or **Maintenance**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add lifecycle details">
    Enter the purchase date and odometer value when known.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and create">
    Check **Basics**, **Assignment**, and **Technical details**, then create the truck.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Truck statuses

| Status | Meaning | Planning guidance |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Available** | Ready for operational assignment. | Assign normally. |
| **On route** | Currently operating a trip. | Check timing before another assignment. |
| **Maintenance** | Temporarily unavailable for operations. | Do not assign until maintenance is complete. |

## Change the assigned driver

Edit the truck, open **Assignment**, and choose another driver or **Unassigned**.
This updates the vehicle record; review existing trips separately before changing
operational planning.

## Keep fleet data reliable

- Update status when a truck enters or leaves maintenance.
- Update the odometer using your team's agreed cadence.
- Edit a registration correction instead of creating a duplicate truck.
- Search by registration or driver before adding a new record.

<Warning>
  Before deleting a truck, check active and historical planning. Referenced fleet
  records may be protected from deletion.
</Warning>
