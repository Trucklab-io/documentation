**Operations** brings post-planning workflows together. It is available to Owners
and Admins and uses approved transport data from the active workspace.

## Access requirements

| Role | Operations access |
| --- | :---: |
| Owner | Yes |
| Admin | Yes |
| Regular | No |

Regular users who open the route see an access-denied view because financial and
subcontracting workflows are restricted to administrators.

## Operations sections

| Tab | Use it to |
| --- | --- |
| **Pré-facturation** | Check readiness, proof, price, and invoice progression. |
| **Alertes** | Acknowledge late, blocked, unconfirmed, or missing-proof work. |
| **Affrètement** | Assign a transport to a carrier and follow the response. |
| **Transporteurs** | Maintain the carrier partner directory. |
| **Suivi client** | Create and revoke time-limited customer tracking access. |
| **Routes & ETA** | Review planned distance, timing, delay, and estimated arrival. |

The **À jour** indicator means the live Operations view is connected. **Reconnexion**
means Affretio is attempting to restore updates. Use the refresh action when you
need to reload all sections explicitly.

## Recommended operating sequence

<Steps>
  <Step title="Approve planning">
    Publish a reviewed version so its trips become active operations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow execution">
    Use Planning and **Alertes** to resolve late starts, delays, blocks, and missing proof.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share or subcontract when required">
    Create a customer tracking link or an affrètement for the selected transport.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete delivery evidence">
    Confirm the transport is delivered and a signed proof is available when required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare invoicing">
    Resolve readiness blockers, approve the pre-invoice, export, then record invoicing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Period filters

Pre-invoicing and Routes & ETA offer **30 jours**, **90 jours**, and **Cette année**
presets plus required **Du** and **Au** dates. The selected inclusive period cannot
exceed 366 days.

<Warning>
  Operations includes financial data and one-time secret links. Confirm the client,
  transport, price, recipient, and selected workspace before sharing or advancing work.
</Warning>
