Open **Operations → Pré-facturation** to review transport records before invoicing.
The page separates records that are ready from records requiring more information.

## Filter the work queue

1. Choose **30 jours**, **90 jours**, **Cette année**, or enter **Du** and **Au**.
2. Choose one client or keep **Tous les clients**.
3. Select **Appliquer**.
4. Use **Tous**, **Prêts**, or **À compléter** for readiness.

The summary shows the number and value of ready records and the number of blocked records.

## Open a transport dossier

Select the row action. The dossier includes:

- route, client, date, and transport identifier;
- effective price;
- readiness result and blocker list;
- invoice status progression;
- delivery receipt details when available.

## Resolve readiness blockers

| Blocker | What to verify |
| --- | --- |
| **Transport non livré** | Mark the trip delivered only after actual completion. |
| **Prix absent** | Add a valid manual price or ensure an applicable tariff grid and distance. |
| **Preuve de livraison manquante** | Obtain the required signed delivery receipt. |
| **Incident non résolu** | Resolve the operational issue before billing. |

See [Billing readiness](/concepts/billing-readiness) for the complete model.

## Follow the invoice lifecycle

| Status | UI label | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pending | **À préparer** | The dossier still needs preparation or approval. |
| Approved | **Approuvé** | Internal checks are complete. |
| Exported | **Exporté** | The record has been exported for the billing process. |
| Invoiced | **Facturé** | The external invoice has been recorded. |

Open a ready dossier and use the next available action. Affretio disables progression
while the dossier is incomplete. Enter the invoice reference when advancing to **Facturé**;
it is required to finish the cycle.

## Verify proof of delivery

A signed receipt can show the recipient's name, capacity, signature state, time,
and any reservations. Treat this as operational evidence and personal information.

<Warning>
  Do not advance a dossier merely to clear the queue. A delivered status, effective
  price, proof, and incident state must represent the real operation.
</Warning>

## Export safely

Apply the intended client and date filters, review the ready queue, and verify the
output before sending it to an accounting system or another person. Export status
does not itself prove that an external invoice was issued.
