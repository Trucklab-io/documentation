Open **Operations → Routes & ETA** to compare planned route information across a
date range. This view is designed for operational review, not live GPS tracking.

## Choose a period

1. Select **30 jours**, **90 jours**, **Cette année**, or enter **Du** and **Au**.
2. Confirm the end date is not before the start date.
3. Keep the inclusive range within 366 days.
4. Select **Appliquer**.

## Interpret the result

The route list can combine:

- transport date and identifier;
- departure and arrival locations;
- client;
- planned distance;
- planned departure and duration;
- latest unresolved delay;
- calculated estimated arrival.

## How ETA is calculated

Affretio derives ETA from the planned trip date, departure time, duration, and
latest unresolved delay. If the required planning fields are missing, no reliable
ETA can be shown.

<Note>
  The page explicitly uses Planning data, not vehicle GPS. Treat the ETA as an
  operational estimate and communicate that limitation when sharing it.
</Note>

## Improve ETA quality

- Enter a realistic departure time and duration.
- Maintain route distance and addresses.
- Record delays promptly and resolve them when no longer applicable.
- Correct the Planning entry instead of compensating with informal notes.
- Use customer tracking when a client needs a controlled external view.
