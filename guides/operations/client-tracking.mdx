**Operations → Suivi client** creates a public, time-limited view for one transport.
The client can see relevant progress without an Affretio workspace account.

## Create a tracking link

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Suivi client">
    Select **Créer un accès**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the transport">
    Select the intended transport by date, route, and client.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set validity">
    Choose 24 hours, 3 days, 7 days, or 30 days.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and copy">
    Select **Créer le lien**. Copy the secret while the banner is visible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send securely">
    Send the link only to the intended client contact and explain its expiry.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the public page can show

The tracking view can include the trip date, cities, execution status, estimated
arrival, latest delay, delivery time, and delivery-receipt outcome.

The share does not grant general workspace access.

## Share states

| State | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Actif** | Link is usable. |
| **Expire bientôt** | Less than approximately 24 hours remain. |
| **Expiré** | Validity period ended. |
| **Révoqué** | An administrator ended access early. |

## Revoke a link

Find the share and select **Révoquer**. Confirm the action. Revocation is permanent
for that URL; create another share if access is later required again.

<Warning>
  Treat every tracking URL as a secret. Do not place it in tickets, public chat,
  documentation, or screenshots. Verify the selected client and transport before sending it.
</Warning>

## If a client cannot open tracking

- Confirm the URL was copied completely.
- Check whether it is Expired or Revoked.
- Confirm the client is using the newest link.
- Create a replacement only after confirming continued access is appropriate.
